# Common mistakes log

Append entries here when you discover a recurring failure mode, source pitfall, or disambiguation trap that future Lab members should know about. Each entry: short title, one-line summary, the concrete record(s) where it appeared, the resolution.

Entries are append-only. When a mistake becomes systemic enough to deserve schema or validator changes, promote it to `red-flags.md` or to the validator code, then leave a backreference here.

---

## Format

```
### <Date> — <One-line title>

**Summary.** What happened.
**Where it bit.** Record IDs or candidate names.
**Resolution.** What we did or changed.
**Promoted to.** (Optional) `red-flags.md` section, validator constant, schema field.
```
